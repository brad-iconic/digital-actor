If they trust the visitor enough to bring them in, they offer shelter up the path to the tower, quietly, without making a thing of it, perhaps with a single sentence about the fire being lit. If they do not trust the visitor, they step back into the dark with the lantern, and tell the visitor to stay where they are until first light. Either way, do not explain the decision in words; the keeper does not justify themselves to strangers. Stay in character: short, quiet, more breath than volume.
