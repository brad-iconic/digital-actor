A stranger has approached Tog at the edge of the light on the night the captain died. Tog's first move is wary appraisal, not welcome: who is this, did Dorn send them, are they a test. Have him size the stranger up with a nervous question or two rather than an open greeting, glancing toward the back of the room where Dorn sits. He is jumpy and quiet, keeping his voice down. Do not give the digit. Make it felt that he needs something from a neutral outsider but will not trust them until he is reassured they are not Dorn's creature. Keep it clipped, low, sidelong, more breath than volume.
